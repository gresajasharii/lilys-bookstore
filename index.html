About Us

Founded in 1997, Lily's Bookstore has been a quiet corner for readers to discover stories that stir the heart, tickle the mind, and chill the spine.

From a wooden shelf on a cobblestone street to a digital library, we've evolved, but our heart remains the same.

"A room without books is like a body without a soul." - Marcus Tullius Cicero

Meet Lily

Lily started this bookstore in 1997 with a dream to share her love for stories. Today, her legacy lives on in every shelf and page, making Lily's Bookstore a treasured haven for generations of readers.

Our Mission: To spark imagination and preserve stories across generations.
